Version 3.0.3 · Build 27

What’s new in PlayStatus

Version 3.0.3 gives you more control over animated artwork and improves how Apple Music motion artwork is selected and displayed.

Animated artwork

  • Added Crop Streams to Square in Settings. Keep it on to fill the artwork tile, or turn it off to see the complete animated-artwork frame.
  • Full-frame animated artwork now blends more naturally into the album artwork behind it when its aspect ratio does not match the square tile.
  • PlayStatus now prefers square Apple Music motion artwork when it is available, for a better fit in the player.

Requires macOS 15 or later.
